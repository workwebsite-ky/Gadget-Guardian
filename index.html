FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all products 100% original? +
Yes, absolutely. Every product we sell comes from authorized distributors and carries the manufacturer's official warranty. We do not deal in grey market or refurbished items unless explicitly labeled.
Do you offer installment plans? +
Yes! We partner with major banks to offer 0% installment plans on select products. Plans range from 3 to 24 months. Visit our store or contact us to check eligibility.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a 7-day return policy for sealed/unused products and a 14-day exchange policy for manufacturing defects. Products must be in original packaging with all accessories.
Do you offer device repair services? +
Yes, our in-store repair lab handles screen replacements, battery changes, water damage repairs, software issues, and more for all major smartphone and laptop brands.
Can I order online for home delivery? +
Yes! We offer same-day delivery within Karachi for orders placed before 3 PM. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to place your order.

Who We Are

Karachi's Premier Electronics Destination

Gadget Guardian was founded in 2015 with a single mission: to give people in Karachi access to genuine, premium electronics without the anxiety of grey-market products or shady retailers.

What started as a small shop in DHA has grown into a full-scale electronics hub serving over 10,000 customers — from students buying their first laptop to professionals upgrading their entire home office.

We believe technology should be accessible, authentic, and supported. That's why every product we sell comes with real warranties, expert advice, and after-sale service you can actually count on.
